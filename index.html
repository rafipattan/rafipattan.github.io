About Me

I am a professional video editor and graphic designer with experience in creating engaging visual content for brands, social media, and promotional campaigns. I focus on clean storytelling, strong pacing, and visually polished edits that capture attention and drive results.

With expertise in Adobe Creative Suite and a passion for visual excellence, I've worked with diverse clients ranging from small startups to established brands. Each project is approached with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to delivering content that resonates with audiences.
